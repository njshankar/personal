Honorable Mention - Liyu Lake (鯉魚潭)

It's really more like a large pond. But it's a very peaceful one. Taking in the calm waters, mountain scenery, cute cafes, boat rental shops, and fairgrounds makes for a pleasant and leisurely afternoon. There are also monkeys and fireflies (in May).
