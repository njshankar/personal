8 - Mugu Muyu（慕谷慕魚）

It was closed for a long time. But one day in March, I discovered that it has re-opened. The main road is still under construction, so I had to ride on a very bumpy dirt road instead. But the discomfort to myself (and my scooter) were both worthwhile. The color of the water is so pure. I have never seen anything else like it.
