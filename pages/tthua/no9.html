9 - Nanan（南安）

I didn't get the chance to climb Yushan this year, but going to Yushan National Park wasn't a bad consolation. I spent a very memorable May afternoon admiring the rice fields by Yuli and walking on the old Batongguan trail, which passes by many waterfalls. On the way back to town, I stopped at Nanan Falls, a tall cascade right by the road.
